The Energy Security Scenarios

As the world recovered from the COVID-19 pandemic it was already facing a challenging set of circumstances , with austerity looming, energy prices rising and security tensions increasing. The Russian invasion of Ukraine amplified many of these challenges and brought security to the very top of the global agenda. The Energy Security Scenarios consist of two scenarios called Archipelagos and Sky 2050. They explore how different country archetypes react to the tensions and trends and ask: "Can a world desperate for immediate security also meet the long-term challenge of climate change?"
